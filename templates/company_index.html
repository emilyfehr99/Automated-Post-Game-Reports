About StrideSync Hockey

Revolutionizing hockey performance through data-driven biomechanics analysis

At StrideSync, we challenge traditional training methods by bringing science, data, and research to the forefront of hockey development. Hockey has long lagged behind other sports in leveraging technology and biomechanical analysis. We're closing that gap to create elite, impactful players.

We operate as a consulting firm, prioritizing constant evaluation of our leadership, team, and methodologies. We stay ahead of trends, integrate new research, and continuously improve. Like our clients, we're in pursuit of excellence and mastery.
